Here are some projects that I've worked on:



Procore - Procore Technologies

I was an iOS development intern at Procore Technologies, a cloud-based construction management software company, from April to June 2014. At Procore, I improved the directory functionality by allowing users to be able to message their contacts directly from the Procore directory. I also integrated this functionality with the messaging software WhatsApp.



NovaConnect - Novacoast Inc.

My senior capstone project was sponsored by Novacoast Inc. where a team four other graduating seniors and I designed an application that allows easy sales lead management to those on trade show floors. My portion was writing the entire mobile application, which included integrating the camera to capture business cards, creating a drawing interface to label various parts of the business cards to be read by OCR software, and JSON files to be sent off to the lead management system.



Calculator - Brandon Newman

Calculator is my attempt to out-Apple Apple in the iOS 7 flat design department. The goal is to go above and beyond the call of duty from Apple's built in calculator, providing support for character deletions, and operations with parenthesis.
